Dlp Slicer

October 2022


We created our own slicer for DLP Printers. We took 3D meshes and used voxelization to convert to points in order to slice up the mesh. Ram was a big issue in voxelization which we combated through using sparse and int8 arrays. The voxelization would be save onto a bitmap
